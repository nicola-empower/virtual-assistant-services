Our Commitment to Your Data Security

The Data We Collect About You

We may collect and process various types of personal data from you, including Identity, Contact, Financial, Transaction, Technical, Profile, and Usage Data, as well as any service-specific data you share with us.

How We Collect Your Data

We collect data through Direct Interactions (when you fill in forms, purchase services, or correspond with us) and Automated Technologies (like cookies on our website). We may also receive data from third parties such as analytics and payment providers.

How We Use Your Data

We only use your data to provide our services, manage our relationship with you, process payments, and comply with legal obligations. We will not use your data for marketing purposes unless you explicitly opt-in.

Data Security

We take the security of your data very seriously. We have implemented robust measures to protect your personal data from accidental loss, unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. These measures include:

  • Secure Wi-Fi Networks & Data Encryption
  • Strong Password Protection (updated every 90 days)
  • Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) where available
  • Up-to-date Antivirus and Firewall Protection
  • Secure Servers, Cloud Storage, and Backups
  • Strict Access Controls (only Nicola has direct access)
  • Regular Software Updates for the latest security patches

Data Retention

We will only retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes we collected it for, including legal or accounting requirements. If your data is not actively used for services, we will not store it for longer than one year.

Your Legal Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have rights in relation to your personal data, including the right to request access, correction, erasure, and more. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.
