How we engineered a bespoke scraping engine to audit and identify businesses with a critical need for web services.

The "Hunt and Peck" Problem

A web design agency was wasting hours manually searching Google Maps and social media to find potential clients. This "needle in a haystack" approach resulted in a cluttered list of leads, many of whom didn't actually need new websites.

Manual Searching

Hours spent browsing maps and social feeds.

Low Relevance

Pitching to businesses with great sites already.

Messy Data

Inconsistent contact info and no tracking.
